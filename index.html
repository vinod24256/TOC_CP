The Process

Three steps.
One Turing Machine.

Step 01

Message enters the tape

Your plaintext is written cell by cell. Read/write head positions at index 1. State initialises to q₀.

┌───┬───┬───┬───┬───┐
│ □ │ h │ e │ l │ l │
└───┴───┴───┴───┴───┘
↑ head @ 1 · state q₀
Step 02

Transitions fire — characters encrypt

δ(q, σ) → (q', σ', D) fires for each character. Uppercase → q_upper · Lowercase → q_lower · Non-alpha → q_skip. Each letter shifts by k.

[q₀] ──'h'/'k',R──▶ [q_lower]
[q_lower] ──'e'/'h',R──▶ [q_lower]
[q_lower] ──'l'/'o',R──▶ [q_lower]
Step 03

TM halts — cipher delivered

On reading blank □, the TM transitions to q_accept and halts. Tape holds the ciphertext. L(M) = Σ* — Total Decider.
