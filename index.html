How TM Crypt works

Three steps. One Turing Machine.

01 STEP 01

Message enters the tape

Your plaintext message is written onto the TM's infinite tape, one character per cell. The read/write head positions at cell 0. State initializes to q₀.

02 STEP 02

Transitions fire, characters encrypt

For each character, δ(q, σ) → (q', σ', D) fires. Uppercase letters enter q_upper, lowercase enter q_lower. Each character shifts by k positions.

[q₀] ──'h'/'k',R──> [q_lower]
03 STEP 03

TM halts — message delivered

On reading blank □, the TM transitions to q_accept and halts. The tape now holds the ciphertext. The language L(M) = Σ* is Recursive.

k h o o r ✓ [q_acc]
